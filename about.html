Our Story

Serving Pilgrims for Over 35 Years

Niharika Bhawan was founded in 1988 with one simple purpose — to give pilgrims visiting Mata Vaishno Devi a clean, safe, and affordable place to rest. What began as a modest guesthouse has grown into one of Katra's most trusted names in hospitality.

The property has welcomed thousands of devotees, families, and solo travellers over the decades. Our commitment to clean rooms, honest pricing, and genuine hospitality has never wavered.

We are located at Main Market, Prashar Gali, Katra, Jammu & Kashmir 182301 — just minutes from the Banganga check post, the official starting point of the Vaishno Devi Yatra.
